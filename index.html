In the framework of the continuous endeavor to protect the authority from cyber risks and raise the level of compliance with the basic controls of cybersecurity issued by the National Authority for Cybersecurity, the authority obtained a compliance rate of 63.3%, an increase of 38% over last year.

This achievement was achieved by the grace of God and then thanks to the cooperation of all departments and employees of the authority in applying some cybersecurity policies and procedures based on best practices and standards to reduce cyber risks, and adherence to which may reduce ﬂexibility and increase some restrictions on some previous practices. The Cybersecurity Department works with the Information Technology Department together to ﬁnd alternatives that achieve ﬂexibility without prejudice to cybersecurity policies and procedures.

On behalf of the Cyber Security Department and the Digital Transformation Sector, we thank all departments for their cooperation and support.
